Neutralization
We have seen that acid contains (H+) hydrogen ions and base contains (OH-) hydroxide ions . Salt and Water are formed by combination of acid and base.
Reactions
Acid + Base → Salt + Water
HCL + NaOH → NaCL + H2O
This Chemical reaction is called Neutralization.
